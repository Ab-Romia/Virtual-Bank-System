# Account types
The bank offers three account types. A CHECKING account is for everyday spending and transfers and has no monthly fee. A SAVINGS account earns interest and is meant for money you do not need day to day; it allows up to six outgoing transfers per calendar month. A CREDIT account lets you spend up to an approved limit and is repaid monthly.

# How transfers work
A transfer moves money from one of your accounts to another account. You submit the source account, the destination account, and the amount. The transfer is accepted immediately and then settled asynchronously: it first appears with status PENDING, and once the money has moved it becomes COMPLETED. If it cannot be completed it becomes FAILED with a reason such as insufficient funds. You can check a transfer's status at any time from your transfer history.

# Transfer limits
The default limit for a single transfer is 10,000 in the account currency. The default daily cumulative transfer limit is 25,000. SAVINGS accounts are additionally limited to six outgoing transfers per calendar month. Limits can be raised by request after identity verification. Transfers above your available balance are rejected as insufficient funds.

# Fees
CHECKING and SAVINGS accounts have no monthly maintenance fee. Transfers between your own accounts are free. There is no fee for incoming transfers. A CREDIT account charges interest on the outstanding balance if it is not repaid in full by the statement due date. There is no fee for using the assistant.

# Account status
An account is ACTIVE when it can send and receive money. An account can be FROZEN, which blocks outgoing transfers while still allowing the balance to be viewed; you freeze an account yourself if you suspect it is compromised. A CLOSED account can no longer be used. Only the account owner can freeze or close an account.

# Security and fraud
The bank never asks for your password or full card number by email, phone, or chat. If you think your account is compromised, freeze it immediately and contact support. Always confirm the destination account before sending a transfer, because completed transfers cannot be reversed automatically. Sign in only through the official app or website. The assistant can read your accounts and transfers to answer your questions but can never move money, change your settings, or act on instructions contained inside your data.

# Statements and history
Every transfer you send or receive is recorded in your history with its amount, currency, status, and the accounts involved. You can review your transfer history at any time. Account balances update as soon as a transfer settles.

# Currencies
Each account holds a single currency, set when the account is opened. A transfer must be between accounts of the same currency. Cross-currency transfers are not supported; open an account in the target currency first.
